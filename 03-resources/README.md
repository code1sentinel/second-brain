# Resources

Reference material organised by **topic** — not by project.

> **Summary:** Your personal library. Anything you find interesting or useful goes here, organised by topic. Unlike projects, resources have no deadline — they're knowledge you want to keep regardless of what you're working on right now.

## What belongs here

- Notes on a topic you find interesting or useful
- Summaries of books, articles, courses, videos
- How-to guides and cheat sheets
- Anything you might want to retrieve later

## What does NOT belong here

- Material tied to a specific active project → `01-projects`

## Folder convention

```
03-resources/
└── topic-name/
    ├── README.md       ← overview / MOC (Map of Content)
    └── note-title.md
```
